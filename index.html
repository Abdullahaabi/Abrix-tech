Digital Signage Platform

We developed an advanced SaaS platform for a leading retail technology provider to revolutionize how businesses manage and display their digital content across multiple locations. Designed for seamless content scheduling and real-time display control, the platform enables brands to enhance customer engagement, maintain consistent messaging, and maximize the impact of their in-store advertising.

Key Features:

  • Dual-Portals Architecture – Admin Portal for onboarding clients and Business Portal for content management
  • Dynamic Content Scheduling – Advanced playlist and campaign scheduling
  • Real-Time Monitoring & Control – Instantly update content across all displays
  • Multi-Format Support – Handles images, videos, animations, and interactive content

Impact: By combining flexible content management with real-time delivery, this platform empowers businesses to streamline operations, enhance brand presence, and run targeted campaigns that increase both engagement and sales.

Manufacturing Optimization Platform

We developed a cutting-edge SaaS platform for a leading industrial consultancy to revolutionize how complex manufacturing plants optimize their operations. Built with a Zero Loss Analysis methodology, the platform helps manufacturers unlock hidden efficiencies, boost output, reduce downtime, and improve margins—without major capital investments.

Key Features:

  • Dual-Portals Architecture – Admin Portal for onboarding companies and Business Portal with Site Manager App and Visualizer App
  • Rich Visualization Tools – Interactive dashboards, Gantt charts, Pareto analyses, and golden cycle tracking
  • Data-Driven Decision Making – Uses historic process data to uncover untapped potential

Impact: By blending empirical data analysis with deep industry expertise, this platform empowers manufacturers to sustain improvements, gain competitive advantage, and achieve up to 5–50% operational performance gains.

Fleet Tracking & AI Optimization

We developed a complete web and mobile SaaS solution for logistics and delivery companies to monitor, control, and optimize fleet operations from anywhere. Combining real-time GPS tracking with AI-powered route optimization, the platform enables fleet managers to cut costs, reduce idle time, and increase on-time deliveries.

Key Features:

  • Web & Mobile Apps – Access fleet data on any device, with native mobile apps for iOS and Android
  • Dual-Portals Architecture – Admin Portal for management and Business Portal for operations
  • AI Route Optimization – Dynamically calculates the most efficient delivery paths
  • Driver Mobile App – Real-time delivery instructions and proof-of-delivery uploads
  • Alert & Maintenance System – Automatic alerts for speeding, route deviations, and maintenance needs

Impact: By integrating real-time GPS intelligence with AI-driven planning, SmartFleet enables logistics firms to reduce fuel usage, improve delivery times, and boost fleet productivity—resulting in higher profits and customer satisfaction.

HR & Workforce Management

We built a fully custom enterprise HR software solution with integrated web and mobile applications, designed to centralize all HR functions in a single platform. Tailored for the client's exact workflows, HRMatrix automates payroll, performance tracking, and attendance while providing employees with a self-service portal.

Key Features:

  • Custom-Built Web & Mobile Apps – Fully responsive web platform and dedicated mobile apps for both Android and iOS
  • Admin & Employee Portals – Admin for management and Employee for self-service
  • Biometric & Mobile Attendance – Integrated biometric scanners and mobile geofencing
  • Automated Payroll – Salary calculation, tax deductions, and bank integrations
  • Performance & Goal Tracking – Customizable KPI dashboards for employees and managers

Impact: As a tailor-made HR solution, HRMatrix eliminated manual data entry, reduced payroll errors, and improved transparency across the client's organization. The result: faster HR processes, happier employees, and measurable cost savings.
